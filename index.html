The problem

Your model learns exactly what your labels teach it.

Automation can label fast, but it can't tell you when it's confidently wrong. Ambiguous cases, edge behavior, nuance, and shifting guidelines are exactly where models fail, and where only trained human judgment holds up. Metabit puts experts at the center: AI pre-labels the routine, people resolve the hard cases, and every label is reviewed, measured, and traceable before it reaches your training set.
